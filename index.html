Dice Game!

Game instructions:


1) Enter in a number of players (2-4) and a number of boardtiles (10-100)

2) Players will take turns rolling dice when it is their turn, the number they roll is how far they advance on the board

3) If they land on a Special tile, they may get an upgraded die or get a chance to move to the end of the board by rolling a percentile die

4) Once a player has reached the end of the board, they win!
